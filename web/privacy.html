Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Story Charts is a hobby project that lets writers visualize story structure as charts. This policy describes what information the service collects, how it is used, and your rights over it. It applies to the website at storycharts.com and the Story Charts apps for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

Who runs Story Charts

Story Charts is operated by James Dai Limited (the "service"). Contact: privacy@storycharts.com.

What we collect

We collect only the information needed to let you sign in and save your charts:

  • Account identifier. When you sign in, our authentication provider (Cloudflare Access) gives the service your email address. We use it as the owner identifier for the stories you create.
  • Your story content. Stories, plots, and scene positions that you create are stored on our servers so you can access them from any device.
  • Basic request logs. Like most web services, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) records standard request metadata such as IP address, user agent, and timestamp for security and abuse prevention. We do not use this data for analytics or advertising.

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, health data, microphone audio, or any device identifiers beyond what is inherent to a standard web request. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We do not show ads.

How your content is shown to others

Story Charts is a shared gallery. Any story, plot, or scene you create is visible to every other signed-in user who browses the service. Please do not upload anything you are not comfortable sharing publicly, and do not include personal, private, or sensitive information in story titles, plot names, or scene labels.

Only you can edit, rename, or delete the stories you create. Other users can view them but cannot modify them. Your email address is used internally as the owner identifier and is not shown to other users on story pages.

We do not read, analyze, train machine learning models on, or sell your story content. The only automated processing is storing it, returning it when asked, and displaying it in the shared gallery.

Reporting content and contacting us

If you see a story, plot, or scene that you believe is abusive, infringing, or otherwise objectionable, email privacy@storycharts.com with a link to the story and a brief description. We review reports and remove content that violates these guidelines, typically within 24 hours. We may also block users who repeatedly post such content.

Where your data is stored

Data is stored using Cloudflare D1 (a managed SQLite database) and Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare acts as our infrastructure provider and processes data on our behalf. See Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy for details on how they handle data as a processor.

How long we keep it

We keep your stories for as long as your account exists. If you delete a story, it is removed from our database. If you want your entire account and all associated content deleted, email us at the address above and we will do so within 30 days.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

Children

Story Charts is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Your rights

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by emailing privacy@storycharts.com. Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR or CCPA) you may have additional rights; we will honor them regardless of your location.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
