Delete your Story Charts account

Story Charts is operated by James Dai Limited. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Delete from inside the app (Android, iPhone, iPad)

  1. Open Story Charts.
  2. Tap the menu in the top right.
  3. Tap Sign Out, then Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all stories are deleted immediately.

Delete from the web

  1. Go to storycharts.com and sign in.
  2. Open the menu and choose Delete Account.

Delete by email

If you can no longer access the app or website, email privacy@storycharts.com from the address associated with your account. We will delete your account within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your email address and account record
  • All stories, plots, and scenes you created
  • All sign-in session tokens

What is retained

Nothing. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Backups are rotated within 30 days.
